Gothenburg Marine Chemical Laboratory (GMCL) is the unofficial name of Dr. Ulfsbo’s research group at the Department of Marine Sciences, University of Gothenburg. The group’s logo and name were inspired by the former Department (and later Division) of Analytical and Marine Chemistry (1979-1999), led by Prof. David Dyrssen - the founding father of Swedish marine chemistry.
