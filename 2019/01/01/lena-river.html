Lena River discharge 1936-2020 Discharge data from Kyusyur station (Lena River) from 1936 to 2020. Colors from blue to red indicate the year from 1936 to 2020. The blue and black thick solid lines show the year 2011 and 2014, respectively, and the black thick dashed shows the mean of the period from 1936 to 2020. The figure is based on Juhls et al. (2020). Discharge data is from The Arctic Great Rivers Observatory.

Juhls, B., Stedmon, C. A., Morgenstern, A., Meyer, H., Hölemann, J., Heim, B., Povazhnyi, V., & Overduin, P. P. (2020). Identifying Drivers of Seasonality in Lena River Biogeochemistry and Dissolved Organic Matter Fluxes. Frontiers in Environmental Science, 8(May), 1–15. https://doi.org/10.3389/fenvs.2020.00053
